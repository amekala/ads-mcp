description: Find and fix wasted ad spend across all platforms
argument-hint: <optional: specific platform>
---
Run a wasted spend analysis across $ARGUMENTS (default: all connected platforms).

1. Call `mcp__adspirer__analyze_wasted_spend` for Google Ads waste
2. Call `mcp__adspirer__analyze_meta_wasted_spend` for Meta Ads waste
3. Call `mcp__adspirer__analyze_linkedin_wasted_spend` for LinkedIn Ads waste
4. Call `mcp__adspirer__analyze_search_terms` to find irrelevant search terms
5. Identify top sources of waste, calculate potential savings, and recommend specific fixes with expected impact
