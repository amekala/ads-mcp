# Multi-Account Management

Adspirer supports managing multiple ad accounts across all platforms from a single AI conversation.

## How It Works

- **Multiple accounts per platform** — Connect several Google Ads, Meta, LinkedIn, or TikTok accounts
- **Switch between accounts** — Use `switch_primary_account` to change the active account
- **Cross-account analysis** — Compare performance across accounts
- **Account-level permissions** — Each account has its own OAuth authorization

## Use Cases

### Agency Management
Manage multiple client accounts without switching dashboards. Ask the AI to pull reports across all clients or focus on a specific account.

### Multi-Brand Companies
Companies with multiple brands or business units can manage all their ad accounts through a single interface.

### Testing & Production
Keep separate accounts for testing and production campaigns, switching between them as needed.

## Example Usage

Switch to my client "Acme Corp" Google Ads account
