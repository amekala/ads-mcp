# Campaign Management

The Campaign Management skill guides the AI through the complete campaign creation workflow.

## What It Does

1. **Research** — Keyword research with real CPC data and competitive analysis
2. **Plan** — Campaign structure, ad groups, and budget allocation
3. **Create** — Build the campaign with proper settings and targeting
4. **Write** — Generate ad copy aligned with your brand strategy
5. **Extend** — Add sitelinks, callouts, and structured snippets
6. **Validate** — Review all assets before launch

## Supported Campaign Types

| Platform | Campaign Types |
|----------|---------------|
| Google Ads | Search, Performance Max, YouTube, Demand Gen |
| Meta Ads | Image, Carousel, Video |
| LinkedIn Ads | Image, Text, Video, Carousel |
| TikTok Ads | Image, Video |

## Example Usage

Ask your AI assistant:

```
Create a Google Search campaign targeting "AI marketing tools"
with a $50/day budget focused on lead generation
```

The skill will walk through keyword research, campaign creation, ad writing, and extension setup — all in one conversation.
