# Privacy Policy

Adspirer respects your privacy. This policy explains how the MCP server handles your data.

## What We Collect

- **Authentication tokens** — OAuth tokens for connecting to ad platforms (stored securely, server-side)
- **Account metadata** — Ad account IDs and names for multi-account management
- **Usage metrics** — Aggregate tool call counts for billing (no content logged)

## What We Don't Collect

- **Conversations** — Your AI chat content is never logged or stored
- **Campaign data** — Ad performance data is fetched in real-time and not retained
- **Personal browsing** — No tracking cookies or behavioral data
- **Third-party sharing** — Your data is never sold or shared

## Your Rights

- **Disconnect anytime** — Remove ad platform connections at any time
- **Delete account** — Request complete account deletion via [support](mailto:abhi@adspirer.com)
- **Data export** — Request a copy of your stored data
- **GDPR compliance** — Full rights for EU users under GDPR

For privacy questions, contact [abhi@adspirer.com](mailto:abhi@adspirer.com).
